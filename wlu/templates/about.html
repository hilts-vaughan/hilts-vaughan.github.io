What?
Study@WLU is a room scheduling application that lets student find empty classrooms around the Waterloo WLU campus. It's powered by the LORIS API written by the same person.
Who?
Study@WLU is written and maintained by Vaughan Hilts, a student from Wilfrid Laurier University.
