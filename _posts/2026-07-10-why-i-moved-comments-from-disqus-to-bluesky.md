I've been using Disqus for comments on this blog since I started it back in 2014. I'm now moving away from Disqus and over to Bluesky for comments. I recently began migrating my blog to a new theme after not having touched it in a very long time. I did the entire migration barely looking at the network tab. When I finally finished the migration and took a peek on mobile, I was _horrified_. I am sure I probably knew Disqus could inject ads before but I didn't realize they were _that_ bad. [Hacker News has some good threads on this.](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=45423268). I have to imagine many of us techies are running this stack without realizing the experience is now awful. Oops!

So I moved the archived comments into the site itself and switched the live discussion over to Bluesky. Antigravity did most of the migraiton but I quickly ran out of tokens on the free tier and had to swap to Codex... after feeding it some of the blog posts around the internet on this various topic. The archive preserves the old thread history, which matters on a blog that has been around for a while (few as the comments may be!). Unfortuantely, it looks like Antigravity
decided to build it's own version of the script rather than use an existing one such as the one I had tried and linked. It did allow things to fit into the blog theme a bit better, so I suppose it's fine.

The entire migration probably took an hour or so and will probably take another hour or two to work out the kinks. Not bad, though!

Not much else to write. Glad to be off.
